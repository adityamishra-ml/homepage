Website Template

Abstract

Welcome to the complete source bundle for my personal website! Inside you’ll find all the HTML, CSS, images, and configuration files I used to build and deploy this site. I’ve taken care to organize everything into a clean, modular structure so you can easily explore, customize, and extend the template for your own projects.

Feel free to clone or download this repository and adapt it however you like—just be sure to keep a link back or a credit to my work in your own project’s footer or README. Whether you’re looking for a simple portfolio layout, a responsive navigation bar, or ready-to-use component styles, I hope this template saves you time and helps you get online faster. Happy coding!
