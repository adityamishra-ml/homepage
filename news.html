Latest News

Invited Lecture at IEEE ICASSP 2025

April 10, 2025

Aditya Mishra delivered a keynote lecture on “EEG Representation Learning with Graph-Convolutional Variational Autoencoders” at IEEE ICASSP 2025 in Hyderabad. The presentation covered the latest advances in neural representation learning techniques for brain–computer interfaces and highlighted future directions for robust EEG-based models.

Mitacs Globalink Research Internship Concluded

August 02, 2024

After a fruitful 12-week internship at Queen’s University in Kingston, Canada, Aditya completed his research on subject representation learning frameworks. The collaboration produced a publication, accepted at the IEEE ICASSP 2025.

Joined Queen's University as Mitacs GRI

May 13, 2024

Aditya joined Queen’s School of Computing in Canada as a Mitacs Globalink Research Intern under the guidance of Dr. Javad Hashemi and Dr. Ali Etemad. The fully-funded Mitacs Globalink program offers outstanding opportunities for students to tackle real-world challenges at leading Canadian institutions. During his internship, Aditya’s research focused on zero-shot inference techniques for EEG data.
