About Me

I’m Aditya Mishra, a machine–learning researcher specializing in Bayesian generative models, representation learning, and applied AI for education. I hold a Master’s from Stanford and plan to pursue a PhD focusing on interdisciplinary research at the intersection of finance and ML.

Outside of research I love playing soccer (I captained my university team!), exploring responsible tourism in India’s national parks, and mentoring students in AI summer camps.
